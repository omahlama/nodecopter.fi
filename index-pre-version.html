Nodecopter Helsinki
Mininodecopter — Sat April 20th

Overview

The first Nodecopter event in Finland is here! We are programming flying robots in Helsinki (or Espoo) on April 20th for the whole day. We'll start at around 9am on Saturday and hack until around 6pm. Then we'll have a demo.

The programming happens in teams of three persons. Each team will receive one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, there is a demo session where each team gets to present their work to visitors and other attendees.

The event will be held at Startup Sauna space in Otaniemi. The address of the venue space is Betonimiehenkuja 3D, 02150, Espoo. The Startup Sauna space can easily be accessed with buses number 102 and 103 from Kamppi.
