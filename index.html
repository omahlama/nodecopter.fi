Nodecopter Helsinki
Mininodecopter — Sat April 20th

Overview

The first Nodecopter event in Finland is here! We are programming flying robots in Helsinki (or Espoo) on April 20th for the whole day. We'll start at around 9am on Saturday and hack until around 5pm. Then we'll have a demo.

This is a hack day. The programming happens in teams of three persons. Each team receives one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spends the day programming and playing with it. Each team decides among themselves what to make. At the end of the day, there is a demo session where each team gets to present their work to visitors and other attendees.
